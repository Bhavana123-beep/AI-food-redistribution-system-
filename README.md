# AI-food-redistribution-system-
🍲 AI Food Redistribution System

An AI-powered platform that connects food donors with NGOs and volunteers to reduce food waste and fight hunger.

📌 Problem Statement

Every day:

Restaurants throw away extra food

Hostels and households waste cooked food

Events waste large quantities of food

At the same time, many people sleep hungry.

This happens not because food is unavailable, but because food is not properly distributed.

💡 Solution

The AI Food Redistribution System connects:

🍽️ Food Donors (restaurants, events, households)

🚚 Volunteers/NGOs

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Needy people

Using AI and smart tracking, the platform ensures surplus food reaches people who need it — quickly and safely.

🚀 Features

🔐 User authentication (Donor / Volunteer / NGO)

📍 Live location tracking (GPS-based)

📦 Food donation posting system

🤖 AI-based priority matching (urgent food → nearest NGO)

🔔 Real-time notifications

📊 Dashboard for tracking donations

🗺️ Map view for nearby food availability

🧠 Where AI/ML is Used

Food demand prediction

Smart matching of donor & receiver

Priority detection (food expiry time)

Route optimization (nearest volunteer)
